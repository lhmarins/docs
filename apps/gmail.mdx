# Gemini in Gmail

Gemini is directly integrated into Gmail to help you manage your inbox more effectively.

## Summarize Long Threads

You can use Gemini to easily summarize a long email thread.

1. Open the email thread.
2. Click on the Gemini icon in the top of your gmail tab. Type 'summarize this email thread'.
3. Gemini will provide a bulleted list of the key points, decisions made, and any pending action items.

<Note>Summaries are private to you and are generated on the fly.</Note>

## Draft Responses

You can ask Gemini to help you draft emails.

1. Click **Help me write** when composing a new email or replying. You can also highlight over an already typed section to receive recommendations.
2. Provide a brief prompt, such as: _"Draft a polite decline to this vendor proposal, mentioning we are focusing on internal tooling this quarter."_
3. Gemini will generate a draft for you to review and edit.

## @Calendar Integration

You can reference your calendar directly within Gemini to check your schedule or draft emails about availability.

<Card title="Using @calendar" icon="calendar">
Use the `@calendar` smart chip in your prompts to let Gemini know you want it to reference your schedule.

_Example: "Check `@calendar` for my availability next Tuesday and draft an email to the team suggesting a time for the sync."_

</Card>
