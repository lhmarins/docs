# Gemini in Google Meet

Stay focused on the conversation while Gemini handles the administrative tasks in Google Meet.

## AI Notetaking ("Take Notes for Me")

You no longer need a dedicated note-taker in every meeting. Gemini can automatically capture notes, action items, and key decisions.

1. Start or join a Google Meet call.
2. Click on the **Gemini** icon at the bottom.
3. Select **Take notes for me**.
4. At the end of the meeting, the notes are automatically saved to Google Docs and shared with the meeting organizer.

<Warning>
**Compliance Reminder**: Always inform participants that the meeting notes are being automatically recorded by AI. Review the generated notes to ensure no highly sensitive material is inappropriately documented if not required.
</Warning>

## "Catch me up"

If you join a meeting late, you can ask Gemini to summarize what you've missed without interrupting the flow of the conversation.

1. Open the Gemini side panel in Meet.
2. The panel will display a running summary of the conversation so far.
3. You can also ask specific questions like: *"Did the team decide on the launch date yet?"*
