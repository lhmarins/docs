# Gemini in Google Drive

Finding the right document or extracting information from multiple files can be time-consuming. Gemini in Drive helps you synthesize information quickly across your workspace.

## Source Referencing

Instead of searching for keywords and opening dozens of files, you can ask Gemini a question directly in Drive.

Gemini will search your files and provide an answer, along with citations linked directly to the source documents.

1. Open Google Drive.
2. Click on the **Gemini** icon in the top right corner.
3. Ask a question, such as: _"What was the key takeaway from last month's all-hands presentation regarding the new remote work policy?"_
4. Gemini will cite the specific Presentation or Doc where it found the answer.

<Note>
  **Data Privacy**: Gemini only searches files you have access to. It respects
  existing sharing permissions and does not use your organization's Drive data
  to train public models.
</Note>

## Summarizing Multiple Documents

You can ask Gemini to summarize a specific set of files.

_Example: "Summarize the project scope mentioned in `@Q3_Project_Brief` and `@Vendor_SLA_Agreement`."_

## Create transcripts

Gemini can create a transcript for a video or audio file. To do this click on the file you would like to create a transcript for and click on the Gemini icon and use the prompt "Generate a transcript for this file"
