# Getting Started with Gemini for Workspace

Gemini for Workspace helps you write, organize, and automate tasks right where you work. Think of Gemini as a collaborative partner that helps you accelerate your day-to-day workflow.

<Warning>
**Privacy Check**: "Am I including data that someone else might expect me to keep private?"

Before interacting with Gemini, always review your prompts for sensitive or confidential information. While Gemini for Workspace maintains enterprise privacy standards, it's a best practice to sanitize data and avoid inputting anything considered highly proprietary or personally identifiable information (PII).
</Warning>

## Why use Gemini?

- **Save Time**: Quickly summarize lengthy documents and email threads.
- **Spark Creativity**: Brainstorm ideas, draft content, and overcome writer's block.
- **Boost Productivity**: Automate routine tasks and get up to speed faster.

## Review Requirements

<Note>
Always verify Gemini's outputs before sharing. You are responsible for the final product, so please ensure that any generated text or data is accurate, appropriate, and adheres to company guidelines.
</Note>
