# Advanced Automation: Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio allows you to create automations within Google Workspace. You can create automations to categorize emails that you need to respond to and have it draft a response in your voice. You can also create an automation to receive a meeting briefing before all your meetings where Gemini will search your Gmail, chat and drive for any context related to that meeting.

The next two tabs will go through a couple of example workflows.

<Note>
  **Extensibility**: Workspace Studio is highly customizable. You can adjust
  prompts and triggers to fit the unique needs of your team.
</Note>
