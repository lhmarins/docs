# Advanced Automation: Workspace Studio

Workspace Studio allows power users to build custom AI-driven workflows that connect multiple Workspace apps together without extensive coding.

## Custom Email Labeling

You can set up rules instructing Gemini to automatically categorize incoming emails based on content, sentiment, or specific project keywords, rather than just relying on simple filters.

1. Navigate to the Workspace Studio interface.
2. Create a new workflow triggered by "New Email".
3. Use a Gemini prompt action to evaluate the email: *"Determine if this email is a high-priority customer escalation, a routine vendor update, or an internal informational memo."*
4. Add a subsequent action to apply the appropriate Gmail Label based on Gemini's categorization.

## Automated Morning Briefs

Start your day with a synthesized summary of what happened overnight.

1. Create a scheduled workflow in Workspace Studio (e.g., "Run at 8:00 AM daily").
2. Connect it to your Inbox and your Calendar.
3. Provide the prompt: *"Summarize the top 3 most important unread emails from my manager or direct reports, and list my meetings for today highlighting any back-to-back blocks."*
4. Output the result into a new Google Doc or have it emailed to yourself automatically.

<Note>
**Extensibility**: Workspace Studio is highly customizable. You can adjust prompts and triggers to fit the unique needs of your team.
</Note>
