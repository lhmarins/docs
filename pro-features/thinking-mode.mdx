# Gemini Pro Features

For complex analytical tasks, Gemini offers advanced features that allow it to reason through problems and conduct extensive research.

## "Thinking" Mode

In 'Thinking' mode, the AI takes more time to process your prompt, break down the problem step-by-step, and evaluate different approaches before generating an answer. This is highly recommended for coding, logic puzzles, or strategic planning.

To activate Thinking mode:

1. Open the Gemini web app (`gemini.google.com`).
2. Toggle the model selector at the top to the latest "Thinking" or "Advanced" model (e.g., Gemini Advanced).
3. The AI will explicitly show its "thinking process" before delivering the final response.

## Deep Research & Canvas

When you require a comprehensive overview of a topic, use the Deep Research feature.

- **Deep Research**: Instead of a simple summary, Gemini will scour the web or your Drive to compile a detailed report with multiple citations and synthesized insights.
- **Canvas**: This is a dedicated workspace for writing and presentations. When you ask Gemini to generate long-form content, it may open a "Canvas" side-by-side with your chat. You can highlight specific sections of the text in the Canvas and ask Gemini to rewrite, debug, or expand just that portion without regenerating the entire output.
